The £660,000 offer is £60,000 (+10%) above the median sold price for 3-bed terraced/semi houses in the subject's own postcode sector (N22 5: median £600,000), comes from a chain-free buyer, and was negotiated up from £600,000. It is above the sector median and higher than 14 of the 19 comparable sales in the subject's sector — the upper end of what these homes actually complete at.
