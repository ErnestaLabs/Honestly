# Market Appraisal: The £660,000 Offer — Test Property

**Property:** 3-bed terraced freehold, **Test Property**, London N22 · stated 160 sqm · purchased **£580,000 (2015)**.
**Current position:** marketed "offers over £675,000"; **accepted-level offer £660,000, chain-free**, negotiated up from an initial £600,000.
**Prepared 2026-06-04 · Source: PropertyData — HM Land Registry sold prices, portal asking listings, market indices. All data pulled 2026-06-04.**

> This appraisal states only what the data shows. Every figure is sourced. No figure is included to persuade; the conclusion is whatever the evidence supports.

---

## 1. The offer, in one line

The £660,000 offer is **£60,000 (+10%) above the median sold price for 3-bed terraced/semi houses in the subject's own postcode sector (N22 5: median £600,000)**, comes from a **chain-free** buyer, and was negotiated up from £600,000. It is **above the sector median and higher than 14 of the 19 comparable sales** in the subject's sector — the upper end of what these homes actually complete at.

---

## 2. The subject's own market history (the strongest single evidence)

This house has already been tested at a higher price — twice:

| Listing | Price | Time on market | Result |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Two separate agents, same price** | **£699,950** | **234 days** | **No sale — neither agent found a buyer** |
| Current agency | £675,000 (offers over) | **44 days** (listed 23 Feb → agreed 8 Apr 2026) | **£660,000 offer secured (chain-free), now SSTC** |

The property's own track record is unambiguous: **marketed at £699,950 by two separate agents, it attracted no buyer across 234 days; repriced to a realistic guide, it produced a £660,000 chain-free offer within 44 days (listed 23 Feb 2026, sale agreed 8 Apr 2026).** Two agents at the same ~£700,000 price could not find a buyer — this is the market's direct verdict on *this specific house*, not an inference from other properties.

*(Note: the property was marketed at £699,950 by two separate agents and did not sell at that price. It is the same property, not a comparable.)*

**What the data shows, stated precisely:**

➡️ **The data does not support £720,000 for this house in this location.** £700k+ is a different-sector outcome; the subject's own market test confirms it.

In a buyer's market averaging 380 days to sell, a chain-free offer £60,000 above sector median, banking a £64,160 net gain over the 2015 price, is the outcome the data backs.

# 市场评估：£660,000 的报价 — Test Property（简体中文）

**报告 2026-06-04 · 来源：PropertyData —— HM Land Registry 成交价、门户要价、市场指数。所有数据调取于 2026-06-04。**

> 本评估仅陈述数据所示。每个数字均有来源。无任何数字为说服而列入；结论由证据决定。

## 1. 报价，一句话

£660,000 的报价比标的所在邮区（N22 5：中位数 £600,000）3 卧排屋/半独立的成交中位数 **高 £60,000（+10%）**，来自**无连环链**买家，且由 £600,000 谈判上调而来。它**高于标的所在邮区 19 笔可比成交中的 14 笔**，并高于该邮区中位数——处于这些房屋实际成交价的高端。

## 2. 标的自身的市场历史（最强单一证据）

这套房子已在更高价位上被测试过——两次：

| 挂牌 | 价格 | 在售时长 | 结果 |

物业自身的记录明确无误：**由两家不同中介以 £699,950 挂牌，234 天内无人接手；调整为现实指导价后，在 44 天内便产生了 £660,000 的无连环链报价（2026 年 2 月 23 日挂牌，4 月 8 日达成销售）。** 两家中介在约 £700,000 的同一价位上都找不到买家——这是市场对*这套具体房子*的直接裁决，并非由其他物业推断而来。

## 4. £720,000 的期望，用数据检验

3 卧排屋/半独立，1 英里内，按邮区段（成交，24 个月）：

**数据所示，精确陈述：**

➡️ **数据不支持这套房子在此区位达到 £720,000。** £700k 以上属于不同邮区段的结果；标的自身的市场测试已予证实。
