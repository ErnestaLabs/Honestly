§ 01  -  The problem

Everyone gives you a price. Nobody proves it.

A valuation you cannot see inside is just an opinion with a pound sign. Whichever side of the deal you are on, that leaves you guessing.

01
If you are selling

The highest number wins the instruction

The agent who quotes the biggest figure often wins your business. That lands the listing - it does not mean a buyer or a lender will ever agree.

02
If you are buying

The asking price is just a hope

It is what the seller hopes for, not what the home is worth. With nothing showing you what nearby homes actually sold for, it is easy to overpay - and spend the next year wondering by how much.

03
If you are an agent

The evidence tools are locked away

Hometrack, Sprift, Acaboom - the data that wins and defends instructions, gated behind a pro account and hundreds of pounds a month.

Honestly is the valuation that shows you why.
