# Landing objection-handling (pre-empt the doubts before they bounce)

Source copy for the rebuilt landing. On-brand: honest, plain, hyphens only (no em dashes).
Each objection = the doubt a real visitor has, and the answer that removes it.

## 1. "Why Telegram? Why not a website or an app?"
Because a valuation is a conversation, not a form. There is no account to make, no
password, no app to download - you almost certainly already have Telegram, or it takes
30 seconds. The whole appraisal (the number, the working, the report, the audio
walkthrough, the map) lands in a chat you keep forever and can re-open any time. It also
means payment runs through Telegram itself, so your card details never touch us.

## 2. "Do I have to pay? Do you want my card?"
Your valuation is free - the real figure, the strict sold comparables with their HM Land
Registry links and the full local facts. No card, no account, nothing to cancel. If you want
more later you pay in Telegram Stars and Telegram handles it; we never see a card number.

## 3. "Isn't this just another Zoopla estimate?"
No. Zoopla and Rightmove hand you one black-box number from an algorithm you cannot
inspect. We show the working: the real sold comparables (each one linked to its HM Land
Registry record so you can check it), the condition adjustment, and the live-market steer.
Every figure is open to challenge. That is the whole point.

## 4. "Why would I trust a bot over an estate agent's valuation?"
An agent's "free valuation" is a pitch to win your instruction. The number is often
inflated to flatter you into signing, or trimmed to sell fast for an easy fee. We have no
instruction to win and nothing to sell you on the back of it. The number is whatever the
sold evidence supports - and you see the evidence, not just the figure.

## 5. "Is my address and data safe?"
We use the address only to pull public sold-price data and build your report. We do not
sell your details, we do not pass them to agents, and there is no "a local expert will be
in touch" call waiting for you. No spam, no funnel.

## 6. "How accurate is it? Who actually built the number?"
It is built the way a careful surveyor reasons: HM Land Registry sold prices as the floor,
condition-adjusted against a market anchor, then steered by what is live on the market
right now. We do not pretend a home has one exact price - no one can. We give you a
defensible central figure and an honest range, and we show how we got there.

## 7. "What's the catch?"
There isn't one. The free valuation is the product working, not bait. If it is useful you
may pay for the deeper reports or the agent tools. If it is not, you have lost nothing and
you keep the free appraisal.
