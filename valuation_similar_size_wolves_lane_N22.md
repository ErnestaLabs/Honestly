## 5. Assessment of the £660,000 offer

Framing: £675,000 is "offers over" (a floor), so the test is where £660,000 sits against the similar-size sold evidence.

| Benchmark (similar/near-size, ≤0.5 mi, 3-bed) | Figure | £660k vs it |
|---|---|---|
| Absolute **median**, Tier A+B (n=4) | £656,250 | **+£3,750 (essentially at median)** |

| Bracketing pair | £600,000 (180 sqm terr) ↔ £765,000 (163 sqm semi) | inside the bracket |

**Verdict: the £660,000 offer is FAIR.** It is within £4,000 of the median similar/near-size sold price (£656,250), sits inside the bracket formed by the two closest-size sales, and is consistent with the similar-size £/sqm estimate (~£642,000). This broadened, like-for-size evidence supports the offer **far better** than the terraced-only / any-size set did (which had topped out at £635k and made £660k look full).

**The revealing counter-evidence, stated plainly (as requested):** a **larger** 3-bed (180 sqm terraced) sold for **£600,000 — £60,000 below the offer**. So the subject's size does **not** automatically justify pushing well above £660k; raw floor area is not converting into proportional price here. The upside case (£765,000) is real but rests on a **semi-detached** (which typically commands a premium over terraced) sold 12–24 months ago. Net: £660,000 is well-supported as fair; the evidence neither marks it as a bargain nor clearly justifies the seller's £675,000 floor, and it gives little support to anything materially above ~£680k for a 160 sqm *terraced* house.

## 6. Limitations & data gaps

1. **Small sample.** Only **2** truly-similar (≥130 sqm) comps; **4** including near-size. Indicative, not conclusive. The size band was widened (not the type or distance rules) and recency extended to 24 months solely because the similar-size 12-month set was < 3.
2. **Recency.** 3 of the 4 sized comps (incl. both bracketing sales' partner and the same-street reference) fall in the **12–24-month** window — flagged in the table. Only 22 Stirling Road (£600k, 180 sqm) is within 12 months.
3. **Type premium.** The £765,000 upper comp is **semi-detached**, generally superior to a mid-terrace; it should not be read as a like-for-like terraced figure.
4. **EPC areas noisy.** 22 Stirling Road's 180 sqm (£3,333/sqm) is probably overstated; its absolute £600,000 still stands as evidence. "End-of-terrace" cannot be isolated in the data and is included within terraced/semi.
5. **Subject 160 sqm unverified** and pivotal. If accurate, the subject is mid-pack on size among these comps; if overstated, value shifts toward the smaller-house evidence (which was higher £/sqm but lower absolute prices). EPC / measured survey advised.
6. **Distance anchor approximate** (subject's exact position on Wolves Lane unknown; anchored on the street's sold coordinates). Stirling Road comps (0.459–0.464 mi) are near the 0.5-mile edge.

## 6. 局限与数据缺口

1. **样本小。** 真正相似（≥130 平方米）的可比仅 **2** 套；含近似面积共 **4** 套。属指示性而非结论性。仅放宽了面积区间（未动类型或距离规则），并因相似面积 12 个月集合 < 3 而将近期窗口延展至 24 个月。
2. **近期性。** 4 套有面积可比中有 3 套位于 **12–24 个月**窗口（表中已标注）。仅 22 Stirling Road（£600k，180 平方米）在 12 个月内。
3. **类型溢价。** £765,000 的上限可比为**半独立屋**，通常优于中段排屋；不应视为同类排屋数字。
4. **EPC 面积有噪声。** 22 Stirling Road 的 180 平方米（£3,333/平方米）很可能被高估；其绝对价 £600,000 仍成立为证据。"端头排屋"在数据中无法单列，已并入排屋/半独立屋。
5. **标的 160 平方米未经核实**且至关重要。若属实，标的在这些可比中面积居中；若高估，价值将偏向较小户型证据（其 £/平方米 更高但绝对价更低）。建议进行 EPC / 实测测量。
6. **距离锚点为近似值**（标的在 Wolves Lane 的确切位置未知，以该街道成交坐标为锚）。Stirling Road 各可比（0.459–0.464 英里）接近 0.5 英里边界。
